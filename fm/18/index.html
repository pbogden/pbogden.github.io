Baby Boomers Are Not Leaving Their Single-Family Homes for Apartments

This chart challenges the perception that Baby Boomers are downsizing from their single-family homes. Available data through 2013, when the housing recovery was two years old, reveal no significant reduction in the rate at which Baby Boomers occupy single-family detached homes.
